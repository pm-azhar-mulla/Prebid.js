Keyword Targeting Configuration

Configure positive and negative keywords to improve targeting accuracy and content relevance.

Targeting keywords help determine the context of content. Positive keywords boost association with related content, while negative keywords prevent association with unwanted content.

✓ Positive Keywords

Add keywords that describe content you want to target. Separate keywords with commas or press Enter after each keyword.

Example: sports, technology, finance, entertainment
sports ✕
technology ✕
education ✕

✕ Negative Keywords

Add keywords for content you want to avoid. Content with these keywords will be excluded.

Example: violence, adult content, politics, controversy
violence ✕
adult content ✕
